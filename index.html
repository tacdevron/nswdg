Branches

Our unit offers various branches to choose from. Each branch provides unique opportunities and roles.

Silver Squadron

A unit specializing in advanced maritime and airborne operations, known for its high-level tactical skills and versatility.

24th Special Tactics Squadron

An elite Air Force unit focused on conducting special operations missions, including combat control, pararescue, and tactical air control.

160th SOAR (Nightstalkers)

A premier Army aviation unit that provides helicopter support for special operations, known for its nighttime flying capabilities and precision.

Central Intelligence Agency (CIA/Missionmakers)

The CIA's division dedicated to creating and planning covert operations and intelligence missions to support national security objectives.
